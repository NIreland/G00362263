Cuphead Wiki - Bosses The Root Pack The first boss level where The Root Pack are fought. One of the two bosses initially available. Unlocks Ribby and Croaks upon defeat. Their level is titled Botanic Panic. This boss has three phases (two on Simple Mode). Goopy Le Grande The second boss level which is where Goopy Le Grande is fought. One of the two bosses initially available. Unlocks Hilda Berg upon defeat. His level is titled Ruse of an Ooze. This boss has three phases (two on Simple Mode). Ribby & Croaks In the third boss level, Ribby and Croaks are fought. Unlocked after beating The Root Pack. Their level is titled Clip Joint Calamity. This boss has three phases (two on Simple Mode). Hilda Berg The fourth boss is Hilda Berg, who is unlocked after defeating Goopy Le Grande. Unlocks Cagney Carnation upon defeat. Her level is titled Threatenin' Zeppelin. This boss has three phases (two on Simple Mode). Cagney Carnation Cagney Carnation is the fifth and final boss of Inkwell Isle One. He is unlocked after defeating Hilda Berg.
